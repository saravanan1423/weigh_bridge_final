Configuration Instructions

  1. Sign in to the Google account used as the sender email.
  2. Open Google Account > Security and enable 2-Step Verification.
  3. Open App passwords from the Security page.
  4. Create a new app password for Mail. Use Weighman WMS as the app or device name if Google asks for one.
  5. Copy the generated 16-character app password.
  6. Enter the Sender Email and use the same full email address as the Username.
  7. Paste the app password into Password / App Password.
  8. Enter the Recipient email address.
  9. Click Save Connection, then Send Instruction Email to verify delivery.
